Ministries

GForce

Raising a Generation Grounded in Grace

GForce is the vibrant youth ministry of Grace Chapter Church, designed for young people aged 14 and above. This is a movement of bold, faith-filled teens who are passionate about discovering their identity in Christ, growing in purpose, and living out the Gospel in their everyday lives. Through engaging teachings, real conversations, fun activities, and community outreach, GForce equips young people to be grounded in the Word, filled with the Spirit, and strong in character. Kids Church

Building Foundations of Faith from an Early Age

Grace Kids is a safe, fun, and nurturing space for children 13 years and below to learn about Jesus and grow in God's love. Our goal is to plant seeds of faith early by teaching foundational biblical truths in creative and age-appropriate ways. With passionate teachers, interactive lessons, songs, stories, and play, we create an atmosphere where children feel loved, valued, and excited to come to church. At Grace Chapter Church, we believe every child is a gift from God, and we are committed to helping them walk confidently in His grace from the very beginning.

#WECARE Campaign

Grace Chapter Church campuses in three cities, Cape Town, Johannesburg and Harare have teamed up to start a campaign to give a hand up to members of the church as well as the community members that are disadvantaged. The Purpose is to serve and provide for those that are in need as the word of God instructs that we should feed the widows and the poor. So here is how it works - We will be collecting non-perishable foodstuffs, blankets, clothes as well as money.

Media

Spreading the Message of Grace

Grace Media is the media ministry of Grace Chapter Church, dedicated to sharing the message of God's grace through various platforms. We produce high-quality content that includes sermons, teachings, podcasts, and videos that inspire and equip believers to live out their faith boldly. Our mission is to reach people far and wide with the transformative power of God's Word, using modern technology and creative storytelling.

Catch all Grace Chapter Church services and teachings live or on replay. We stream services regularly, and rebroadcasts are available for those who miss them.
