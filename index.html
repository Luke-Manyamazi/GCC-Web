Welcome to Grace Chapter Church

At Grace Chapter Church, we believe in the Word of God and everything it declares about us. Our faith is in Jesus Christ, the Son of the one and only living God. Our mandate is to reveal Christ to the world. We serve God by serving people in our communities through equipping and empowering them by the Message of Grace resulting with them resting in Christ, revealing Christ and reconciling the world to Christ.

Our Campuses

Grace Chapter Church is a global family with vibrant campuses in Johannesburg, Cape Town, Harare, the United Kingdom, and Canada. Each campus is uniquely positioned to serve its local community while staying connected to the unified vision of revealing Christ to the world.
